Spatial Discretisation

Think of the fluid as many thin horizontal layers stacked on each other. Each layer drags the one next to it due to internal friction (viscosity).

Instead of a continuous velocity field \(u(y)\), we assign each layer \(i\) a single velocity \(u_i\):

\[ u(y) \approx \{u_1, u_2, \ldots, u_n\} \]
